All-in-One POS Platform

Streamline operations and enhance customer satisfaction with our all-in-one digital solutions. From POS and Kiosks to Mobile Apps and Digital Menu Boards, OrderSune offers everything you need to boost efficiency and engagement. Simplify your digital needs and drive growth effortlessly.

Point of Sale (POS)

Experience unparalleled efficiency with our robust POS system. OrderSune’s POS integrates state-of-the-art software and cloud capabilities, delivering a fast, user-friendly interface that handles high order volumes effortlessly. Manage sales, inventory, and customer interactions seamlessly from anywhere.

Kitchen Display Systems (KDS)

Maximize kitchen efficiency with our intelligent KDS. Display orders digitally in real-time, reducing errors and accelerating food preparation. Ensure timely, accurate order fulfillment to delight your customers.

Self-Service Ordering (Kiosk)

Elevate the customer experience with our intuitive self-service kiosks. Reduce wait times and labor costs as customers effortlessly place their own orders, enhancing satisfaction and operational efficiency.

Online Ordering (Web & Mobile Apps)

Expand your reach and increase sales with OrderSune’s seamless online ordering solutions. Our web and mobile apps offer convenience to your customers and streamline your order management process.

Social Media Ordering

Unlock new sales channels with our social media ordering integrations. Allow customers to place orders directly through platforms like WhatsApp, increasing convenience and engagement.

Digital Menu Boards

Captivate your audience with dynamic digital menu boards. Easily update menus and promotions in real-time, enhancing visual appeal and driving upsell opportunities.

Call Center Ordering

Optimize phone order management with our call center ordering system. Integrate phone orders with other sales channels for a streamlined, unified order processing experience.

Loyalty Program

Boost customer retention and increase sales with OrderSune’s customizable loyalty programs. Reward repeat customers and encourage frequent visits to maximize lifetime value.

Payments

Enhance transaction speed and security with our comprehensive payment solutions. Support for both online and offline payments ensures a smooth checkout experience for your customers.

Inventory Management

Prevent stock shortages and reduce waste with our precise inventory management system. Track stock levels in real-time and streamline your supply chain operations.

AI-Powered Product Recommendations

Increase average order value with personalized, AI-driven product recommendations. Delight customers with suggestions tailored to their preferences, boosting engagement and sales.

Table-Side Ordering / BYOD / QR Ordering

Offer ultimate convenience with table-side ordering options. Enable customers to order from their own devices using QR codes or BYOD functionality, reducing wait times and enhancing satisfaction.

Advanced Reports/Analytics

Make data-driven decisions with our advanced reporting and analytics tools. Gain valuable insights into your operations, helping you optimize performance and drive growth.

Aggregator Integrations

Expand your reach and streamline order management with integrations to leading delivery platforms and aggregators. Simplify your multi-channel sales operations.

Coupons & Discounts

Attract new customers and retain existing ones with our efficient coupon and discount management system. Launch targeted promotions to drive traffic and increase sales.
